Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is an Italian savory dish adored by many. The classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with Noodles and Chesse alternatively and bake until golden perfection. It is great for parties, sunday brunch and even a large family. It freezes and reheats well and the leftovers can keep you satisfied for days. Its exquisite taste and smell is truly a cuilinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Lasagna Sheets
  • Olive Oil
  • Chopped Onion, Garlic, Carrot
  • Cheese Blend
  • Oregano and Chilli Flakes
  • Crushed Tomato Puree
  • Basil and Parsley
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, add the ground beef. Use a spoon to break up the meat and cook until browning.
  2. Add the chopped onions and garlic and continue cooking.
  3. Next, add in salt, pepper, oregano and the chilli flakes as per your liking.
  4. Then add the tomato puree, stir cover and let it cook for 10 mins.
  5. Before cutting off the heat tear in some fresh basil and parsley.
  6. Preheat an oven to 200C, take a greased baking tray and alternatively layer the sauce, cheese and the boiled lasagna.
  7. Cover and bake for 40 mins. Serve hot when it is completely baked.
